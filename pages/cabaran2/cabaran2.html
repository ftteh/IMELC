Please use the formula below to help you in the calculation of carbon emission.

A usage of 1kWh equals to an emission of 0.694 kg CO2 (GreenTech Malaysia, 2014)

For an example:
A usage of 156 kWh per month
1 kWh release 0.694 kgCO2
CO2 emission= 156 kWh x 0.694 kgCO2/ kW =108.264 kgCO2
